Live mapping for real-time fleet tracking

A GPS fleet tracking solution that offers a range of mapping features, such as live, interactive maps, zoom-in capabilities and continuous location data allow you to optimize how you track your fleet in real time.

MAP VIEW IN ACTION

Fleet Director’s Map View option provides a variety of interactive features and location management tools, including live, interactive maps, traffic data, multi-view mapping and on-demand vehicle locates right on the map. This intuitive tab gives you full visibility into vehicle locations and activity—regardless of your fleet size or type. With our user-friendly software and location detection tools, easily eliminate the "Where are my vehicles?" questions.

EFFICIENCY WITH DETAILED MAP DATA

Understanding you're driver's route, driving conditions and behavior can greatly assist in providing a safe environment for them. Having detailed mapping data also allows you to see traffic conditions and overall routes to help ensure that your driver gets to the destination on time. This not only keeps productivity levels high but it also keeps customers satisfied.
